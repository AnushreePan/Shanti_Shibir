India is a non-profit organisation, supported by hundreds of volunteers across various states of India and around the world. Even if unable to join full-time, many volunteers dedicate their time and energy by offering some sort of seva working from their residence.


Shree Krishna tells Arjun in Bhagavad Gita (Verse 4.26):


पत्रं पुष्पं फलं तोयं यो मे भक्त्या प्रयच्छति |

तदहं भक्त्युपहृतमश्नामि प्रयतात्मन:||


Patraṁ puṣhpaṁ phalaṁ toyaṁ yo me bhaktyā prayachchhati tadahaṁ bhaktyupahṛitam aśhnāmi prayatātmanaḥ||


“Seva” loosly translates to “selfless service” in English. JKYog is currently maintaining and developing many projects including the JK University, Hospital, Temples; along with many projects in forms of Web and App Development, Audio-Video Editing, Proofreading, Translation of various texts, etc.
